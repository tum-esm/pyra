# How to Become a Contributor

1. Please read the sections [**Repository Organization**](/docs/developer-guide/repository-organization) and [**Testing and CI**](/docs/developer-guide/testing-and-ci)
2. Look for your feature idea/bug fix on our [**issue tracker**](https://github.com/tum-esm/pyra/issues)
3. Submit an issue if your contrbution idea is not being worked on yet [**using our issue templates**](https://github.com/tum-esm/pyra/issues/new/choose)
4. [**Reach out to us**](/docs/intro/contact) if you want to discuss your ideas or want to add your own solution
