# Contact

## Authors

Pyra was developed by the [Associate Professorship of Environmental Sensing and Modeling](https://www.ee.cit.tum.de/en/esm/home/) at the [Technical University of Munich](https://www.tum.de/en/). Patrick Aigner and Moritz Makowski have implemented Pyra 4 from the ground up.

## Bug Reports & Feature Requests

You can track things we are actively working on GitHub: https://github.com/tum-esm/pyra/issues. We gladly accept bug reports and feedback on Pyra. Feel free to ask us any questions about possible features or discuss similar research endeavors.

Planned updates are listed in the [Roadmap](/docs/developer-guide/roadmap).
